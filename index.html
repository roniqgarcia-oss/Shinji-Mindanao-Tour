About

Shinji Ueda is a half-Japanese, half-Filipino DJ whose sound blends electronic textures, break-driven energy, and global rhythms into high-impact dancefloor sets. Influenced since 2013, he officially debuted in March 2022 through the TIKI Crew — developing sharp crowd awareness, pacing, and adaptability across environments. Comfortable as opener, peak-hour, or closer. Sets run 60–120 minutes.
